About

Taraaweeh Rakaat Counter helps you keep track of how many rakaats you have prayed during Taraweeh. Simply tap "I've prayed 2" after each set of 2 rakaats. After every 4 rakaats, the app will remind you to recite the dua that is traditionally read during the breaks in Taraweeh prayer.

In memory of my late uncle, may Allah grant him Jannat al Firdous.

Save to Home Screen

  1. Open this page in Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Give it a name and tap "Add" in the top right.

The app will now appear on your home screen and open full screen like a native app.
